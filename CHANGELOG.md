## 0.0.1

* Initial release.
* Added `ParallaxSceneView` and `ParallaxSceneLayer` for website-style scroll scenes.
* Added `ParallaxImage` for card/list image parallax.
* Added `ParallaxScrollView`, `ParallaxSection`, and `ParallaxLayer` for layered scroll sections.
* Added an example app demonstrating a CSS-inspired parallax scene.
